Regional Market (Typology A)

Architectural Narrative

Concept: Serving as the cultural and ecological heart of the Highline Oasis, the Regional Market creates a symbiotic loop between urban commerce and bio-fabrication. By integrating 3D-printed PETG microalgal facade systems, the building acts as a living filter.

Architecture: The design utilizes a parametric space frame to optimize structural efficiency and fluid dynamics of the Spirulina bioreactor tubes. The undulating canopy is optimized for maximized solar absorption during the day, which then supports the facade’s oxygen and thermal regulation functions at night.

Technical Specs

Area 3,750 m²
Allocation 15%
Structure Space Frame
System Microalgae (Spirulina)
Material PETG/3D Print
